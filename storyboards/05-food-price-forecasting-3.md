# Food Price Forecasting — Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

## Video Description
Transition to a dark analytics dashboard. Years of price data scroll past — a blue line charting historical food prices. Then the line continues in green — the forecast. It curves upward weeks before the actual spike. Key inflection points glow. A policy maker studies the chart, picks up a phone.
