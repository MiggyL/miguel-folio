# DTR System — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A large office. Hundreds of employees badge in at the door each morning. A wall of monitors shows attendance dashboards — who's in, who's late, who's absent. This is the Daily Time Record system. It tracks every clock-in and clock-out for the entire company.

### Script
"Every morning, hundreds of employees clock in. Every absence, every late arrival — it all flows through one system. The Daily Time Record."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
Behind the scenes: a team of five developers around a table — whiteboards filled with diagrams, Jira boards packed with tickets. Three seniors, one junior, one QA. They've maintained this system for years. The codebase is massive and complex — no documentation, no wiki. The knowledge lives in the code itself.

### Script
"A team of five kept it running. Three senior developers, a junior, and a QA lead. Years of work poured into a complex system — with no documentation and no wiki. The knowledge existed only in the codebase."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
A new person sits down at a desk. No onboarding doc. No walkthrough. No communication from the existing team. He opens the repository alone. Thousands of files. He starts reading — line by line, module by module. But he's not alone. He has AI.

### Script
"Then Miguel joined. No handoff. No communication from the team. Just the raw codebase — and AI by his side. With tools like Claude and Copilot, he started decoding the system on his own."

---

## Segment 4 (0:27–0:36)

### Video Description
The system shows a tabular view — rows and columns of dates, times, employee names. Functional but flat. Then a new screen builds beside it: a calendar layout. Time-in, time-out entries appear in day cells. Colour-coded. Editable directly on the calendar. Managers click a day and adjust entries visually. Both views live side by side.

### Script
"The system had a solid table view. Miguel took it further — he designed and built a full calendar alongside it. Editable time entries, colour-coded, intuitive. A major enhancement he envisioned and delivered himself."

---

## Segment 5 (0:36–0:45)

### Video Description
The new feature goes live. The calendar view is already being used by managers across the floor. Miguel at his desk — AI tools open alongside his code. One person, amplified by AI, delivering what a full team maintained. Camera slowly pulls back.

### Script
"No docs. No help. One developer, powered by AI, delivered a major feature on a system that took five people to maintain. That's the future of engineering — and Miguel is already there."
