# YouTube Q&A Tool — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A developer at her desk. She needs to learn how to configure a Kubernetes cluster. She finds a YouTube tutorial — 47 minutes long. She hits play. Watches for a minute. Skips ahead. Rewinds. Scrubs the timeline back and forth. The answer is somewhere in there but she can't find it. Ten minutes of searching for a ten-second answer.

### Script
"A 47-minute tutorial. The exact answer she needs is buried somewhere inside. She scrubs forward, rewinds, scrubs again. Ten minutes gone — and she still hasn't found it."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
She opens a new tool. Pastes the YouTube link. The full transcript loads instantly — every word, timestamped. She types her question in a search bar: "How do I configure the ingress controller?" The transcript highlights the exact passage. A timestamp link appears. She clicks — the video jumps to the precise moment. The answer plays.

### Script
"She pastes the link, types her question. The tool pulls the entire transcript and highlights the exact answer — with a timestamp. One click, and the video jumps right to it."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
She asks another question. Another instant answer from the same video. Then she pastes a different video — a conference talk. Same thing. Questions, answers, timestamps. Her browser has five tabs of YouTube videos, and she's querying all of them like a search engine. She leans back, satisfied. Learning faster than ever.

### Script
"Every video becomes a searchable knowledge base. Ask questions, get answers, jump to the source. Learning at the speed of curiosity."
