# Food Price Forecasting — Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

## Video Description
Camera pulls back from the market into a global view. Supply chain lines pulse across a world map. A drought icon appears in Southeast Asia. A shipping delay flashes in the Pacific. The ripple effects travel — red lines reaching markets in other countries. Prices climbing everywhere.

## Script
"A drought in one country. A shipping delay in another. These events ripple across supply chains, and by the time prices spike at your local market, it's already too late."
