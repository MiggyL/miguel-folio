# DTR System — Segment 4 (0:27–0:36)

## Video Description
The system shows a tabular view — rows and columns of dates, times, employee names. Functional but flat. Then a new screen builds beside it: a calendar layout. Time-in, time-out entries appear in day cells. Colour-coded. Editable directly on the calendar. Managers click a day and adjust entries visually. Both views live side by side.

## Script
"The system had a solid table view. Miguel took it further — he designed and built a full calendar alongside it. Editable time entries, colour-coded, intuitive. A major enhancement he envisioned and delivered himself."
