# Sheets-to-Form Automation — Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

## Video Description
An office. A woman opens a Google Sheet — five hundred rows of employee data. Names, IDs, addresses, departments. Her job: enter each row into a web form on another tab. She copies the first cell. Switches tabs. Pastes. Clicks the next field. Copies again. The clock on the wall shows 9 AM.

## Script
"Five hundred rows of data. Each one needs to be copied from a spreadsheet into a web form — field by field, row by row. That's her entire day."
