# RPSLS Game — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A corporate event. "Digital Data Day" banner across the stage. Professionals in business attire sitting at round tables. Slides on screen — charts, KPIs, data trends. Polite attention but low energy. People checking their phones under the table. A typical conference afternoon.

### Script
"Digital Data Day. The afternoon session. Charts on screen, polite applause, and half the room quietly checking their phones. The energy was fading."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
The big screen changes. A game interface appears — five icons: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock. Adaptive cards pop up on everyone's phones through the chat platform. Confused looks — then someone taps Rock. A result flashes. Laughter erupts. More people join. The scoreboard fills up. People are standing now, trash-talking, cheering.

### Script
"Then the screen changed. Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock — playable right from their phones. Within sixty seconds, the whole room was competing and laughing."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
The game winds down. People are smiling, talking to each other — strangers who are now connected. The scoreboard shows a winner. Applause — real applause this time. The RonnieAI platform logo briefly visible. The afternoon session that was dying is now the highlight of the day.

### Script
"Strangers became competitors. Competitors became connections. A simple game on a conversational AI platform turned the lowest point of the conference into its highlight."
