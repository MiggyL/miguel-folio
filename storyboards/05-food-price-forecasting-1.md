# Food Price Forecasting — Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

## Video Description
A busy open-air market. Vendors arranging produce — rice, vegetables, cooking oil. Families shopping with careful budgets. Price tags visible. A vendor reaches up and changes a price — higher than yesterday. A mother hesitates, puts the item back on the shelf.

## Script
"At the market, prices change without warning. A kilo of rice costs more today than last week. Families feel it first — and they have no way to prepare."
