# Interactive Resume — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A hiring manager's desk. A stack of printed resumes. She picks one up, scans it, drops it. Picks another — same format, same bullet points. She sighs. Dozens more in the pile. They all blur together. She pushes the stack aside and rubs her eyes.

### Script
"She's read forty resumes today. They all look the same. Same format. Same buzzwords. None of them memorable. None of them alive."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
Her screen lights up — a link in her inbox. She clicks. A website loads. Instead of text, a digital avatar appears. It blinks. It looks at her. Then it speaks: "Hi. I'm Miguel. Ask me anything about my experience." She pauses — then leans forward, intrigued.

### Script
"Then one resume didn't just sit there. It introduced itself. A digital avatar, looking at her, ready to have a conversation about the person behind the page."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
She asks questions out loud. The avatar responds naturally — lips synced perfectly, voice confident. She switches the language toggle to German. The avatar seamlessly switches — same fluency, same personality, different language. She asks about cloud experience, certifications, projects. It answers everything.

### Script
"She asked about cloud architecture. It answered. She switched to German. It kept going — same confidence, same depth. Not scripted answers. A real conversation."

---

## Segment 4 (0:27–0:36)

### Video Description
Camera pulls back to reveal the system — the avatar at the centre, with glowing threads reaching out to three AI models: Llama, Gemma, and Mistral. ElevenLabs generates the voice. Sora renders the face. Claude and ChatGPT power the intelligence. All working together behind one seamless conversation.

### Script
"Behind the avatar — three language models thinking together. ElevenLabs for the voice. Sora for the face. An orchestra of AI, invisible to the person asking questions."

---

## Segment 5 (0:36–0:45)

### Video Description
The hiring manager closes the other resumes. She bookmarks the page. She picks up the phone and dials. The avatar on screen smiles gently and returns to idle. The paper resumes sit untouched. She chose the one that spoke.

### Script
"She closed the other forty resumes. Picked up the phone. The one that talked back — that's the one she remembered."
