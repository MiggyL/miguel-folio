# PPE Detection (Thesis) — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A construction site at dawn. Workers arriving, putting on hard hats, reflective vests, safety goggles. A safety officer checks a clipboard — everyone accounted for. But the site is massive. Cranes, scaffolding, heavy machinery. One person can't watch every corner.

### Script
"Construction sites require full protective equipment at all times. But with hundreds of workers across a massive site, one safety officer can't watch everyone."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
Deep in the site, a worker removes his helmet — it's hot, he's tired. He walks toward a beam being lifted by a crane. No one sees him. The camera sees him. A red bounding box snaps around his head. The system identifies: missing hard hat. Alert fires instantly.

### Script
"It only takes one moment. A helmet comes off because it's hot. No one notices — except the camera. It detects the violation the instant it happens."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
A phone buzzes in the site manager's pocket. She pulls it out — Telegram notification: "⚠️ PPE Violation — Zone C — Hard Hat Missing." Photo attached. She looks up toward Zone C and calls out on the radio. The worker stops. Puts his helmet back on. Crisis averted before it began.

### Script
"The site manager's phone buzzes. Telegram. Zone C. Hard hat missing. She radios in. The worker stops. Helmet goes back on. It took eight seconds."

---

## Segment 4 (0:27–0:36)

### Video Description
End of the workday. Workers leaving safely — all equipment on. The safety dashboard shows the day's log: 3 violations detected, 3 resolved in under a minute. Zero incidents. The site manager closes her laptop. The sun sets behind the cranes. Everyone goes home.

### Script
"Three violations caught. Three resolved in under a minute. Zero injuries. Everyone goes home safe. This wasn't just a thesis — it was built to protect lives."
