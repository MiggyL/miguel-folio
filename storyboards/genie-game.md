# Genie Game — Storyboard

## Segment 1 (0:00–0:09)

### Video Description
A large auditorium. The Inspire event. Hundreds of attendees filling seats. A stage with dramatic lighting. The event organiser backstage, looking at the schedule: "Genie Interactive Experience." The screen is dark. The audience settles in. Anticipation builds.

### Script
"The Inspire event. Hundreds of people in the audience. On the schedule — a Genie experience. Not a presentation. Not a demo. A performance."

---

## Segment 2 (0:09–0:18)

### Video Description
The stage lights shift to blue and gold. A digital Genie character materialises on the big screen — animated, expressive, larger than life. It looks at the audience. Then it speaks — a rich, natural voice, lips perfectly synced to every word. The audience gasps. Children in the front row lean forward, mesmerised. It doesn't feel like a screen. It feels alive.

### Script
"The Genie appeared. It spoke — with a real voice, perfectly synced lips, and expressions that felt human. The audience didn't see technology. They saw a character come to life."

---

## Segment 3 (0:18–0:27)

### Video Description
The Genie interacts with the crowd — responding, joking, performing. The audience laughs, claps, engages. Then the performance ends. Applause fills the auditorium. Camera pans backstage — one laptop. ElevenLabs open in one tab, CapCut in another. One person's setup. Miguel closes the laptop. The audience is still talking about what they just saw.

### Script
"Behind the curtain — one laptop. One person built the voice, the face, and the performance. The technology vanished. All the audience took home was the wonder."
